Rules of the Court

  • Assigned Caseload: Each group will be assigned specific cases to litigate.
  • Order of Proceedings: Groups must answer questions strictly according to the provided sequence.
  • Counsel Collaboration: Groupmates are permitted to assist their active representative by sending suggestions in the chat box.
  • The Clock: A strict 10-second timer will commence the moment the host finishes reading the question.
  • Scoring System: The winning group is determined by total points. Each correct answer awards 1,000 points.
  • Health Bonus: Each group starts with a Health Bar worth 1,000 bonus points, which is added to the final score. Incorrect answers deduct 250 points from this bar.
  • Objection & Steal: If the active player fails to answer within 10 seconds, opposing groups may steal the question for 500 points. The floor is given to the first person to visibly raise their hand.
  • The Verdict (Prize): The victorious group with the highest total score will be awarded a cash prize of ₱350 🤑
